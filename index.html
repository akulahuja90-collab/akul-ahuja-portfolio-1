Projects

L&T Finance – Stock Options Strategies & M2M Analysis

Analyzed and implemented multiple stock options trading strategies on L&T Finance options expiring on 24th February, focusing on optimizing risk–return outcomes under varying market conditions.

The study evaluated directional strategies (long calls and puts), spread strategies, and volatility-based approaches such as straddles and strangles, incorporating market trends, volatility assumptions, and technical as well as fundamental indicators.

Additionally, conducted a mark-to-market (M2M) simulation of L&T Finance futures contracts (October 2025 expiry), tracking daily and cumulative P&L, margin requirements, and collateral movements for both long and short positions. Results were visualized using tables and charts to assess trader returns and risk exposure.
